Notes and presentation slides

  • On the classification of Quantum Cellular Automata (PDF)

    There exists an index theory to classify strictly local Quantum Cellular Automata in one dimension, where Matrix Product Unitary Operators, Quantum Circuits and Quantum Cellular Automata are equivalent notions. It has been recently proved that an extension of this index theory (including torsion) fully classifies Quantum Cellular Automata in two dimensions.
